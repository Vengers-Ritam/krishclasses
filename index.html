Mathematics

Mathematics is the art of finding solutions within constraints, whether by solving an equation, determining a limit, or uncovering the unknowns in a system of variables.

Physics

Physics is the science of understanding the natural world, from the smallest particles in an atom to the vast galaxies in the cosmos, uncovering the forces and laws that govern all matter and energy.

Chemistry

Chemistry connects the microscopic world of atoms and molecules with the macroscopic world we experience, revealing how small changes at an atomic level can lead to significant transformations in materials and energy.
